Hi! I'm Emily, and I'm currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science with an Asian Studies minor at Kennesaw State University. My passion lies in software engineering, web development, and data science. Currently on the pathway to find out which field I love the most and would like to do for the rest of my life. I thrive on immersing myself in challenging projects, connecting with professional individuals, and picking up new skills whenever I can!


Outside of my career, I've been competing in the VALORANT competitive scene since 2021. I currently play for my school's team as their team captain, and I also coach a team from Western University (London, Ontario). If I had asked myself 5 years ago if I would see myself as a leader in the future, I'd say no. VALORANT was the birthplace of my leadership, team collaboration, and management skills. Due this day, I am very grateful to have competed in this game because not only did it allow me to grow as a developer, I grew as an individual.


I love working with people and giving to the community - read more about my experience below!
